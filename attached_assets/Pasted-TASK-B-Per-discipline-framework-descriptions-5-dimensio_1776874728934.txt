TASK B — Per-discipline framework descriptions, 5-dimension hover tooltips, reasoning-signals tab cleanup, §6.4/§6.5 completeness verification
Context
Two UX affordances are missing that directly shape how a professor trusts the tool. First, every framework in the registry has a single conceptualDescription_en/es field; the picker tooltip shows the same text whether SWOT appears under Strategy or under Marketing. Professors need discipline-specific explanations: SWOT-in-Marketing surfaces the brand/positioning angle, SWOT-in-Strategy surfaces the competitive-positioning angle. Second, the five academic dimensions in DecisionDimensionsEditor appear as unlabeled dropdown options — the professor can pick "analytical" without knowing what it means or how it affects the generated case. Third, testing log T-003B shows the reasoning-signals tab still renders "no specific evidence extracted" placeholder rows and has occasional language leakage on T1/MCQ turns. Fourth, §6.4 and §6.5 (mergeReflectionAnalytics coverage of new fields, signal extractor structured output with confidence + marginal evidence) must be verified and completed.
